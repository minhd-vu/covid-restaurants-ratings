Whats COVID-Rating?

COVID-Rating is a platform where businesses will be rated by their prevention practices. Consumers rate a restaurant based on its measures to stop the spread of COVID-19. These reviews can be accessed by other health conscious consumers to ultimately help decide on where to eat. Especially as restaurants are reopening, it is imperative that consumers are well educated on the restaurant's preventative practices. COVID-Rating provides other helpful information relating to COVID like providing live traffic reports and parking/walking space situation. COVID-Rating caters to the new preference and demand
